Who we are

To engage, inform, and equip a new tribe of young Africans who see problems as opportunities in need of solution, and have the poise and wherewithal to solve problems, and transform them into successful projects and businesses that will develop their society and economy, and improve their own living standard.
Our products are organized into 03 sections:

Initiative

this component includes: the problem bank, the solutions hub, match-making (linking innovators to town halls that have problems, then these town halls to donors who are interested in solving their problems by exploiting the solutions or project ideas suggested by innovators), innovator competitions (problems=opportunities) and support

Training

this component includes all the courses and training offered at Solutionneurs: PMP, Project Design, MEAL, Business English, Digital Marketing, Business Development, Business Acumen, Persuasive Writing, Organizational Development, Operations Management and Power Skills.

Consultation

it is all the consulting services for entrepreneurs, startupers (for their projects) and anyone wishing to be oriented for an interview or more broadly for their professional growth

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • The PMP certification exam is conducted in which languages ?

    Our students write the certification exam in the language of their choice: French or English

  • When will the next PMP training session take place ?

    Each year we organize 4 sessions of PMP training:
    1st session: February to March
    2nd session: May to June
    3rd session: August to September
    4th session: November to December

  • Is there a specific date to take the exam or is it according to the candidate ?

    The PMP certification exam is open to anyone who can prove they have taken 35 hours of Project Management courses. Each candidate can compose it as soon as he feels ready.

  • How will the PMP certification be for me useful in my professional field ?

    The PMP certification will make you a reference among your peers in how you perform your various business projects in addition to you gain credibility with your employees. The PMP training and certification will allow you certainly to gain competence in insurance in the exercise of your function.You will be advised as to the best processes adapt, so you can avoid the most common errors. most common and limit losses to your company.

  • Are your training courses online or on-site ?

    Since our main target is made up of professionals in sectors various, we try our best closer to them by limiting as much as possible the temporal and spatial constraints. We give each learner the possibility of choosing the formula that suits him best. So, according to that he/she wishes, we give our lessons in our premises (located at Nouvelle Route Bastos) or in online on Google Meet.

  • What is One-on-One training ?

    This is a formula for which a student is followed by a trainer directly. this trainer constitutes his direct interlocutor and he can move for the learner as needed, within the framework PMP training. With this formula the learner can have personalized follow-up and work in an accelerated way with the trainer

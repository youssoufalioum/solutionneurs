2015 Anzisha Prize Launch In Cameroon
26/03/2015

Solutionneurs Initiative will be hosting the 2015 Anzisha Prize Launch in Cameroon. This is an opportunity to showcase and empower young African entrepreneurs from Central, West and Francophone Africa. The Anzisha Prize is a $75,000 award for Africa's youngest entrepreneurs between the ages of 15 to 22. The Award is an initiative of the MasterCard Foundation and hosted by the African Leadership Academy.

Youth As A Solution Seminar
21/03/2015

The "Youth As A Solution" Seminar organized on March 21st brought together over 50 young leaders and entrepreneurs in Cameroon to exchange and share best practices on the public and economic environment in Cameroon with students from the Havard University. Held at the Hotel Sawa in Douala, it create one of the most positive and energetic atmospheres for movers and shakers in Cameroon. The event was organized by Solutionneurs Initiative in partnership with Inspire Afrika and 'Nous Sommes 1'

#‎MTNMSFTCloud‬ Conference
November 2014

Harambe-Cameroon's Founder and current advisor, Olivia Mukam, was invited by MTN and Microsoft Corporation to intervene in the 4th Panel of the ‪#‎MTNMSFTCloud‬ Conference, to discuss "Investors and ‪#‎Startups‬ - Challenges and Opportunities in the digital age." . Mukam shared the stage with a young business owner, a venture capitalist and an elder in the ICT field.

Days: How to Do Business in Cameroon?
June 2012

Objectives: Provide training, information and stimulation to aspiring and current entrepreneurs on how to develop and realize a business in Cameroon.. The 145 we trained (in both cities) during the first edition, and the 70 (in Douala) during the second edition, i.e. 215 in total, all provided positive feedback on the seminar..

Power Hour Meetup Between Ismael Nzouetom, Founder Of I-Dispo, And The Local Startup/Tech Community
26/03/2015

Inspire local entrepreneurs to aspire to be great entrepreneurs. Provide a networking platform for entrepreneurial Cameroonians, within the country and abroad. Organized 2 networking power hours : In Douala and Yaounde. 20 youth entrepreneurs, start-up owners/co-founders, social activists were present in Douala. 30 young professionals, students, and entrepeneurs present in Yaounde.
